## Overview

Good headings are essential for organizing information and engaging users. They
clearly communicate the content's topic, making the page scannable and easier to
navigate. Effective headlines also improve accessibility for screen readers and
aid search engine optimization (SEO) by defining content relevance.

#### Typographic scale and hierarchy

These text styles are the recommended styles for headings.

| **Category**   | **Description**                                               |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Heading H1** | Primary page titles and largest headers.                      |
| **Heading H2** | Used for secondary headlines.                                 |
| **Heading H3** | Used for tertiary headlines.                                  |
| **Heading H4** | Commonly used for section headers and large accordion titles. |
