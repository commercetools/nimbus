## Overview

Tooltips offer concise, non-obtrusive guidance and supplemental information,
enhancing user understanding and interaction with UI elements.

### Placements

Tooltip placements can populate in different areas, please chose a direction
that will be most visible for the users. and the elements that may be
interacting around them. Options include bottom left, bottom center, bottom
right, top left, top center, top right, left top, left center, left bottom,
right top, right center, right bottom.
